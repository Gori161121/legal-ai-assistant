Legal AI Assistant is designed as a legal operations and document intelligence platform.

### Document Intelligence Center

- contracts

### Legal Research Panel

- knowledge retrieval

- AI assistance

### Analytics Dashboard

- workflow performance
- document activity
- legal operations metrics

- professional legal interface
