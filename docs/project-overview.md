# Project Overview

Legal AI Assistant is designed as an intelligent legal workflow and document intelligence platform.

The system combines AI, legal document management, workflow automation, knowledge retrieval, and decision-support capabilities to improve operational efficiency in legal environments.

---

## Main Purpose

The main purpose of this project is to reduce repetitive legal work, improve document accessibility, accelerate information retrieval, and support legal professionals through AI-powered assistance.

---

## Key Areas

- legal document organization
- legal knowledge retrieval
- contract analysis support
- document summarization
- client intake workflows
- workflow automation
- legal research assistance
- decision-support summaries
- internal knowledge management

---

## Target Users

- law firms
- legal consultants
- compliance teams
- corporate legal departments
- legal operations teams
- legal researchers

---

## Long-Term Vision

Create an intelligent legal ecosystem where legal knowledge, documents, workflows, automation, and AI work together to support faster and more efficient legal operations.
